Video Editing

I have taken on small video editing jobs as hobbies and side projects for friends amongst more stable employment.

Tools I regularly use:

OBS video recording software for PCs.

Elgato Software is used for capture cards especially footage of older games.

Shotcut originally learnt in After Effects but have since made the move due to shotcut being free.

Blendr for when I need 3D models, Krita for digital painting and manipulation, Graphics Gale for 2D sprite creation / animation and, Inkscape for vector drawing. These are all free or open source versions of popular software I have used in the past; Photoshop, Illustrator, 3DS Max, Maya etc.

Portal Trailer

during my time as a carer, I assisted a service user with a YouTube series about Portal that he wanted to make. This was the video that I got the most creative control over. I created the 3D model at the beginning as well as the end-cards (using the creator's directions), as well as editing and timing all cuts. The voice is not mine.

Portal Series
